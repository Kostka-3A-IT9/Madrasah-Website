Project وعي ( Awareness )


Our project is all about raising campaigns and uploading posters publicly about the Institution and it's agenda. We came with this proposal as we know their problem will affect the lives and future of Muslim children and we want to help them through uploading posters and digital advertisement to inform people.

Intervention

This proposed project solution is better than the other alternatives such as volunteering, because this gets a larger scale of people to notice help. This project would need a bit more help than just four students from Grade 9 and will need some money for the paper, for printing, for the campaigns (websites), we can also help by posting online for many people to see.

Rationale

This project is very important as it will help the Institution a lot especially the students. It helps in terms of supplies in materials used for education and funds and it will have a possibility that people will try to volunteer. By doing this, we ensure that the kids that don’t have anything can still be educated for them to have a brighter future.


Deliverable

The success of our proposed project will be measured when our project's mission is done and delivered well which is about raising campaigns and to put up posters. The smart objective of our project is that our main goal is to provide aid and support as much as we can for the Muslim children that are in dire need of help.

Objectives

The main goal of the project is to raise awareness to people about the issue and how they can help.

The specific objectives of the project are:

- To make awareness posters.

- Upload it online.

- Tag all the people you are friends with.

- Ask them to share it.

Outcomes


The Institution will have a supplier of materials which will help the students a lot.

The students will have materials they need for school and personal matters.

The student would have a better education because they have the materials they needed for their learnings.

Outputs

In our project outputs, it needs to have an idea in the institution that can capture people’s attention and be interested. It would be encouraging to have an idea in the institution because this would help the students in their educational journey which will help them in the future. their educational journey which will help them in their future endeavors.

Success Indicators

MADRASAH is an educational Institution that the government acknowledges and keeps in mind. Unfortunately, there isn't much advertising going on to continue to promote this institution to the general public. We can influence others to address the challenge by spreading awareness using our digital skills and tools.

Our project proposal is to help promote the partner institution of Ma-a by using our modern utilities and connections. We can raise our concerns to our local government through some people and ultimately help their situation become better. We may also do this through volunteering our services to the branch.

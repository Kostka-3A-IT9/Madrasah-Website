MISSION

The Madrasah is a community-based institution that provides education in Arabic and Islam language. It aims to serve as an avenue for fostering peace and understanding between Muslims and Christians. The intended outcomes of the program are to develop a sense of identity with their beliefs and values.

HISTORY

In 2004, President Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo gave Executive Order No. 283 or An Order Creating a Madrasah Development Committee. City Government of Davao has given its full financial support to the ongoing Madrasah Centers. Genuine standardization of program structure and procedures is required.

Organizer

Madrasah Education can be used to spread a peaceful and well-developed society, populated and inhabited by God-fearing constituents who are submissive and obedient to Allah's will. The Madrasah must play a critical role in this process, primarily by investing in the social, moral, intellectual, and religious education of Davao's Muslim youth.

BENEFICIARIES

A Madrasah typically gives young Muslims a religious foundation in Qur'anic recitation and Islamic values. This program is not only targeted for the Muslim youth, but it is also welcome to Muslim adults. All are welcome to embrace the culture they have been practicing for centuries while still being an upstanding Muslim citizen.
